My Favorite Heroes

Captain America

Steve Rogers was a scrawny kid from Brooklyn who just wanted to serve his country. Choosen for the Super Soldier Program, Rogers became Captain America and helped fight against the Nazis and Hydra. After saving the world, Captain America sadly became frozen for over 70 years. He would go on to join the Avengers. His powers include super strength and durability.

image taken from https://www.marvel.com/characters/captain-america-steve-rogers

Thor

The God of Thunder made his first comic book appearance back in 1962. Thor is the from the magical realm of Asgard and is the brother of Loki and son of Odin. Thor is a founding member of the Avengers alongside Hulk, Iron-Man, Ant-Man, and the Wasp. Thor's powers include super strength, flight, and lighting manipulation

image taken from https://www.cinemablend.com/news/2493594/thor-4-what-we-know-so-far-about-love-and-thunder

Nightwing

Dick Grayson was the first person to take on the mantle of Robin. After years living in Batman's shadow, Grayson felt that he had out grown the role. Grayson would go on to protect Gotham City as Nightwing. Nightwing has been as a member of the Titans and the Justice League. While Nightwing doesn't have powers, he is one of the best hand to hand fighters on Earth.
